Simple Pricing

PonyUp is free to use, but has a few processing fees:

When someone sends you money:

We charge a 3% fee plus an additional 30¢ anytime someone sends you money

So if someone sends you $20. Then we charge:

  • $0.60 for processing
  • Plus an additional $0.30

  • When you transfer money to your debit card:

    We deduct 25¢ anytime you transfer money out of your PonyUp account and in to your debit card.

    If the transaction fails, then we deduct $1 from your balance.
